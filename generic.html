A little about me

Hello All! My name is Somto, pronounced (SOME-2). My name is of Nigerian Origin and I moved to the United States as a kid with my family when I was six years old. I grew up just North of the Boston area and have loved living there ever since. When I was in the 8th grade my dad gave me the very first iphone as a gift. This was what really sparked my interest in technology. I found the iphone apps very intriguing and would download and play with dozens of them. When I graduated highschool, it was a no brainer to study Computer Science. I wanted to learn how those phone apps worked and how I could possibly build them. Now fast foward a few years and I'm happy to say that I have been able to do just that and am still learning and improving along this path.
